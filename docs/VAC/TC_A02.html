Test Case A 02: HCP from G2 applies and records second dose of vaccination (hepatitis A).

Precondition

A vaccination for hepatitis A is already found in the patient's vaccination documentation which is stored in the ehealth ecosystem. The documents of Test case A 01 are a prerequisite. Any vaccination check carried out with the available data indicates a second dose of hepatitis A.

Sequence

The practitioner HCP1 who administered the vaccination for hepatitis A to the patient creates an ImmunizationAdministration document A-D4-HCP2-C2 with the data on the applied immunization and loads it up to the ehealth ecosystem of the patient.

Result

The newly delivered second dose of the hepatitis A vaccination can be found in the patient's EPR (Result. Dokument A 02).
