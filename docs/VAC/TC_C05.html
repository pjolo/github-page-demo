Test Case C 05: DOES NOT validate patient entry for past illness

Precondition

The patient has created a ImmunizationAdministration document with a past illness entry for Measles.

Sequence

The practitioner HCP1 of the reference community C1 checks the immunization status of the patient and sees the entry for past illness. He will not confirm the entry.

Result

Documents

  • Document ImmunizationAdmininstration by Patient (C_D6_P_C1)
  • Document ImmunizationAdmininstration by HCP1 (C_D7_HCP1_C1)
  • Result. Document C 05

Entries

  • Past Illness Condition by Patient (TCC05_UNDILL1)
  • Past Illness Condition by HCP1 refuted (TCC05_UNDILL2)
  • Past Illness Condition as merged result (TCC05_UNDILL3)
